orbit watchface ( animated and configurable )

--
To configure use Garmin Express or Garmin Connect Mobile 
--
Background - 13 colors
Date Color - 13 colors
Hour Color - 13 colors
Min Color - 13 colors
Sec Color - 13 colors
Digit Color - 13 colors
Animation time - 0 to 10 secs
Arcs - on/off
Arcs rounded - on/off
Width size - 1 to 20
Simple mode on low - on/off
Hands - on/off
Orbits - on/off
Digits - on/off
Status (bat and bt) - on/off
Font size - Auto, XTiny, Tiny, Small, Medium, Large
